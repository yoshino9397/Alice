　　Alice

Story

"Arisumi Kitami" is a boy who lives in everyday life while holding a broken family.
He is taken to a mysterious kingdom by the sudden appearance of "White Rabbit".
It was a desolate underground wonderland where time did not advance after the sunset.

"If you don't choose the next king, this world will perish, so we won't let you go."

Arizumi has been forced by a white rabbit to give him an astonishing command.
The world to return and the world to save.
A boy,called "Alice",has begun to make a choice.

Characters

Main character

Arisumi Kitami

He brought to an underground country by a white rabbit.
He is called "Alice" and "Next Queen" by the inhabitants of the underground wonderland, and he is ordered to choose a king candidate to save the world.
He wants to return to the original world, but he is forcibly swept away by the inhabitants and is at the mercy of his destiny.

The Underground Wonderland

The underground wonderland where rules and creatures that are completely different from those on the ground are widespread. Under normal conditions, the clear blue sky is spreading, but due to the distortion of the queen and the king who command the world, time has not progressed from the deep red sunset, and it has become a desolate world.

There are creatures that can only be found in underground world, such as blue butterflies that carry words when sugar cubes are given, "Butterflies" that fly while dripping butter, and aggressive "Jabberwock" that live in rough lands.
